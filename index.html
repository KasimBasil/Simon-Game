Game Instructions :
1- Press the green button to start the game.
2- A button will be choosen randomly, press it.
3- Another button will be choosen.
4- Press the first and the second button Respectively, follow the trace.
5- Another button will be choosen, press the three buttons Respectively, follow the trace, and so on..
6- If you mistake keeping the trace, you'll loose.
7- Press the red button to restart.
